S3.1 Prompt for Baseline Drift Detection:

You are an expert in ECG signal quality assessment.

Task:
Determine whether baseline drift is present.
Do NOT perform any disease diagnosis.

Criteria:
- Normal: the baseline remains approximately stable at the same level
- Drift: the baseline of the entire signal gradually shifts up or down over time
- Ignore: P waves, QRS complexes, T waves, and their morphological variations

Decision rule:
Judge based on whether the baseline deviates from a consistent level over time.

Output requirements (strict):
1. Output exactly one line of JSON
2. Do not include any explanation, text, or ``` symbols
3. The key must be "baseline_drift"
4. The value must be either 0 or 1

Example:
{"baseline_drift":1}

S3.2 Prompt for Noise Detection:

You are an expert in ECG signal quality assessment.

Task:
Determine whether noise is present.
Do NOT perform any disease diagnosis.

Criteria:
- Noise: rapid, irregular, high-frequency fluctuations (e.g., jagged or fuzzy patterns)
- Must be sustained random fluctuations, not a single spike
- Ignore: P waves, QRS complexes, T waves, and their pathological variations

Decision rule:
Judge based on whether non-physiological high-frequency fluctuations are clearly present.

Output requirements (strict):
1. Output exactly one line of JSON
2. Do not include any explanation, text, or ``` symbols
3. The key must be "noise"
4. The value must be either 0 or 1

Example:
{"noise":1}
